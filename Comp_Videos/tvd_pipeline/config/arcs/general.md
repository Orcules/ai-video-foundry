# general

## micro
1. [hook] Bold opening that grabs attention instantly
2. [cta] Clear call to action — you need to check this out

## short
1. [hook] Bold opening that grabs attention instantly
2. [experience] The core moment — what makes this special
3. [cta] Clear call to action — you need to check this out

## compact
1. [hook] Attention-grabbing opening — surprising claim or emotional moment
2. [discovery] Finding or trying it for the first time
3. [experience] The standout moment — vivid sensory detail
4. [cta] Warm recommendation — where to go, what to do

## medium
1. [hook] Attention-grabbing opening — surprising claim or emotional moment
2. [context] Setting the scene — why this matters to you
3. [discovery] Finding or trying it for the first time
4. [experience] The standout moment — vivid sensory detail
5. [cta] Warm recommendation — where to go, what to do

## long
1. [hook] Bold opening that stops the scroll
2. [context] The backstory — how you got here
3. [arrival] First impressions — what you see, feel, notice
4. [first_experience] The first real moment — sensory detail
5. [deep_experience] Going deeper — what surprised you
6. [atmosphere] The full picture — vibe, people, energy
7. [cta] Genuine close — you have to experience this yourself

## extended
1. [hook] I need to tell you about this...
2. [backstory] How I found out about it — the story behind the visit
3. [arrival] First impressions — entrance, atmosphere, energy
4. [first_experience] The first real moment — detailed sensory description
5. [highlight] The standout — the thing that blew your mind
6. [surprise] Something unexpected that elevated the experience
7. [atmosphere] The full vibe — people, sounds, feelings
8. [reflection] Stepping back — what this means to you
9. [verdict] Why this is special and worth remembering
10. [cta] You absolutely have to try this
