2. [cta] You NEED to try this place — name drop, location, go NOW

## short
1. [hook] Mouthwatering first impression — a dish, a sizzle, "wait till you see this"
2. [experience] The taste, the texture, the moment of biting in
3. [cta] You NEED to try this place — name or location drop

## compact

2. [arrival] Walking in, the vibe, the ambiance, first impressions
3. [taste] First bite — vivid sensory description of the standout dish
4. [cta] Genuine recommendation — where it is, why they need to go

## medium

## long
1. [hook] Bold opening — I found the best [food] in [city]
2. [context] How you heard about it, why you were skeptical
3. [arrival] Walking in — describe the space, decor, energy
4. [first_taste] First dish arrives — vivid sensory explosion
5. [deep_experience] More dishes, drinks, the flavors that surprised you
6. [vibe] The whole package — staff, music, atmosphere
7. [cta] Warm close — you have to experience this yourself

## extended

4. [starter] First course — presentation, first taste

7. [drinks] The drinks — cocktails, wine, signature beverages
8. [atmosphere] The full experience — people, vibe
9. [verdict] What makes this place unforgettable
10. [cta] You absolutely have to go
